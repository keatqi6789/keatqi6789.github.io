WHAT IS YOUR WEEKEND PLAN?

Do you want to have a meaningful and enjoyable weekend? Want to become a baker, make-up artist, hairstylist, photographer, memory expert in a weekend of time? Interested in baking but does not have time to learn? Interested in learning make up but due to time constraint? Interested to find a knitting course but clueless where to find an instructor? No worries! My Weekend Plan helps you to fulfill your dream and become a profession in a weekend!

BAKING

My Weekend Plan offer a variety of hands on baking classes such as Bread Baking Class, Cupcake Baking Class, Cookies Baking Class, Pie Baking Class, Macaron Baking Class and etc.

COOKING

We have the diversified cooking classes that encompass Western Style, Thai Style, Chinese Style, Malay Style, French Style, and Nyonya Stylefor all levels of learners. We provide beginner cooking class for those who never cook or dont know how to cook at all and also specialized cooking class for those who want to improve their cooking skills.

FLOWER ARRANGING

My Weekend Plan offer flower arranging class for professional floral designer to special interest classes for personal enjoyment. Our flower arranging class will provide you a strong basic on the technical skills of flower arranging such as colour, design and composition.

PHOTOGRAPHY

My weekend plan offer you courses ranging from beginner to professional with option of grouping training session or one-to-one workshop. We also provide wide range of classes such as wedding photography, events photography, studio photography, product photography commercial photography and so on.

KNITTING

We provide hands on knitting classes for all skills levels such as newbie knitters, and knitter-wanna-bes, and for lapsed knitters who need a refreshments. Parenting knitting class is also provided for you and your kids to learn knitting together and experience the joy of knitting.

CLAY CRAFT

Our clay craft class is designed to teach you the specific techniques for students of different skill levels in order to create a beautiful clay model easily. Our fun clay craft activities may range from kids to adult or even parenting class.

BRUSH LETTERING

We offer Brush Lettering 1 on 2 private class which guides you through the tools and techniques of this lovely art form from A to Z. At the end of the class, you will bring home a canvas artwork hand lettered by you!

NAIL ART

We have nail art specialists who run their nail art business to provide professional express nail art class to you and yet we have many venues and dates on offer in KL and all around Malaysia. Through our nail art classes, you will learn basic nail art including nail structure, tools of nail, colouring, filling & buffing, various nail art design, rhinestone and sticker design.

MAKEUP & HAIRSTYLING

We have professional make-up and hairstyling expert from this field to give interactive and personalized makeup and hairstyling lessons, a unique teaching style is offered to teach on application techniques and how to achieve desired looks.

AMIGURUMI CROCHETING

We offer basic amigurumi classes which brighten up your upcoming weekends ! Come to experience crocheting one of your own creativity and lovable doll expression and fashion on your figurines or home deco !

PUBLIC SPEAKING

Looking for a solution for stage fright? We provide all levels of public speaking courses to train your skills and help you to develop and deliver your best presentation ever. Suitable for all beginners.
